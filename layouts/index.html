What we do

Heavy timber & glulam

Exposed structural timber and glue-laminated assemblies — long spans, post-and-beam, the joinery that has to read clean from the finished floor.

Mass timber & CLT erection

One of a small handful of Texas crews that handles mass timber erection. Worked on The Soto and other landmark Texas mass timber buildings as the erection sub.

Commercial wood framing

Including on-site truss construction — uncommon scope most framers won't touch. Repeat framing sub on HEB store builds across San Antonio.
