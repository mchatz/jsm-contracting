## A working history

Tom started in commercial construction in 1976, ran a commercial framing business through the 1980s and 90s, and founded JSM in 2006 to focus on the work he does best — projects built around timber. The same lead carpenters have been on his crews for most of that history.

## What JSM is for

Most general contractors don't bid heavy timber or mass timber, and the few that do don't always self-perform the erection. JSM does. Structural engineers in Texas know us as the sub who can be trusted with exposed structure, complicated framing packages, and on-site truss work that other crews pass on.

JSM self-performs heavy timber, glulam, mass timber, and commercial framing scopes. On JSM-led general contracting jobs, the rest of the trades are coordinated by Tom directly.
