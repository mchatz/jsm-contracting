## Specialties

- **Heavy timber framing** — exposed post-and-beam, long-span beams, traditional joinery on spec.
- **Glulam** — engineered beams, arches, and trusses; rigging and setting.
- **Mass timber & CLT** — erection sub on landmark Texas mass timber projects.
- **Commercial wood framing** — including on-site truss construction.
